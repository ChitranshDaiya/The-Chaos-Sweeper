New Chapter
The Highway


You decide to head toward the Bridge of Evermore. After all, it’s the only place with a name that doesn’t sound like a deathtrap. But walking? Nah, that’s not your style. You need a cab or something equally fancy.

I have no intention of walking. Must find a cab or something.

Just as you finish that thought, a bright yellow cab zooms toward you and you wave it down like you’re auditioning for a role in a movie about desperate taxi hailing. The cab screeches to a halt right in front of you.

Yo, man! Need a ride?
Whoa! I can’t believe I just met Bumblebee in real life!
Bumblebee? Who’s that? I ain’t no Bumblebee! I’m Zippy, the professional taxi.
Oh, is that so? Alright, Zippy the professional taxi, can you give me a lift to the Bridge of Evermore?

Zippy bursts into laughter, the kind that makes you question your life choices.

Do I look like a charity worker to you, man? I’m a taxi! I charge for my services. And for the Bridge of Evermore? That’ll be 500 bucks, cash.
C’mon, man. Don’t you think that’s a bit steep for a sweet cab like you?

Zippy’s windshield flickers, and a holographic map pops up in front of you. The route is insanely long.

Look at this, man. It’s at least a 10-hour ride. So I ain’t talking no less than 500 bucks. And don’t try to butter me up—I’m already yellow.
How about this? 400 bucks cash, and you can pick up someone else too?

Zippy ponders for a moment, his digital gears visibly turning.

Alright, deal. But don’t puke—I charge extra for that. Now hop in.

You climb into the cab and settle in the backseat. So far, so good. And honestly, things aren’t too weird. Just an Autobot—or maybe a Decepticon—driving you around.

Hey, Mr. Do you want some entertainment? I got options!



You’re enjoying the ride when suddenly, Zippy slams on the brakes.

(To a samurai who just appeared out of nowhere) Hey there, sir! Need a ride?

The samurai turns, his sharp eyes glancing over the cab before settling on you.

Well, well. Aren’t you Zippy? Heard a lot about you. And who’s this gentleman in the back?
Oh, him? No clue. Some weirdo, I guess. Anyway, hop in.

You swear you see a tiny grin flicker across the samurai’s face as he slides into the seat beside you. The cab rolls forward again.

So, traveler. New around here?
Yep! completely new. But hopefully, I won’t take too long. Maybe three days, tops.
Oh? Must be here on important business. Perhaps I can assist?
No thanks, brother. I got this… (whispers) maybe.

The samurai frowns for a moment. Something about him feels off.

You know, sudden changes can be disorienting.
Sudden… changes?
Yes. You’ve seen things shifting around here, haven’t you? Strange occurrences? Unexplainable events? Maybe you’d like to talk about it?

Okay, now you’re convinced he knows something about the chaos. But instead of pushing the conversation, you decide to keep your lips zipped. You pretend to yawn and drift into sleep.

Your eyes snap open as you hear the samurai’s panicked voice.

(frantic) I’m begging you! Don’t take the highway! Take the dirt road! Unless you want your body riddled with holes!

You groggily look ahead. A massive, swirling orange portal has opened up, and marching through it is… wait, is that an army of chickens with guns?!

Sir, this detour will take longer. Damn those little animals!

The cab veers off-road, narrowly avoiding the poultry militia.

Alright, I officially have no idea what’s happening anymore.

Just as you start feeling safe again, the ground trembles. A massive ostrich stomps onto the road, blocking your path.
The samurai instantly reaches for his katana, his eyes locking onto you.

Here, take this! I can’t handle that thing alone!

He tosses you a gleaming sword. You grip it tightly.
Well. This is happening.
You step out of the cab. Brave? Stupid? Maybe both. But one thing’s for sure—you’re about to fight a giant bird.
